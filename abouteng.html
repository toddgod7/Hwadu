A. Characteristic

Our staffs are professional and fluent in Japanese or English

Bright, spacious and stylish lobby, lounge and cafeteria

guestrooms with classic and elegant design

Providing daily European-style buffet breakfast from 6:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. (to 10:00 a.m. on Holidays)

Free wireless internet access is available throughout the hotel

Providing customers in a long-term contract the free laundry service, exclusive of dry-cleaning

Providing free shuttle bus to Garden Night Market. (Biggest night market in Taiwan)

Free cookies and coffee in lobby.

Two computers with multi-language system in business center.

Underground car parking with 24-hour video surveillance
